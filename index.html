About Impact IQ

Understanding How Corporate Projects Work

Impact IQ is an interactive simulation platform designed to help students and fresh professionals understand how modern corporate IT projects operate.

Instead of learning theory alone, users step into professional roles within project teams and face realistic workplace scenarios.

Disclaimer

Impact IQ is a role-based simulation game developed for educational purposes. The scenarios presented in this platform are fictional situations inspired by challenges commonly found in corporate project environments.

The decisions, outcomes, and feedback within the simulation are designed to help users explore how different professional roles influence team collaboration, project delivery, and organisational outcomes. These results are intended for learning and reflection and should not be interpreted as guaranteed outcomes in real workplace situations.

By continuing, you acknowledge that Impact IQ is an interactive learning simulation created to explore decision-making in professional environments. Real organisations, projects, and team dynamics may vary depending on context, organisational policies, and individual judgement.
