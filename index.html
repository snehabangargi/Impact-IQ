About Impact IQ

What is Impact IQ?

Impact IQ is an interactive simulation platform designed to help students and fresh professionals understand how modern corporate IT projects operate.

Many graduates enter their first job without fully understanding how project teams collaborate, manage requirements, or resolve workplace challenges.

Instead of only learning theory, players experience corporate project environments where they must make decisions that affect project delivery, team collaboration, and stakeholder outcomes.

Disclaimer

Please review before continuing

Impact IQ is an educational simulation developed for learning and training purposes. The scenarios presented within the platform are designed to illustrate common situations that may occur in corporate and IT project environments.

These scenarios are simplified representations intended to support understanding of decision-making, teamwork, communication, and cross-cultural collaboration within project teams. The outcomes and feedback provided in the simulation are illustrative and do not represent guaranteed or definitive real-world results.

Impact IQ does not provide professional, organisational, or management advice. The platform is intended solely to encourage reflection, critical thinking, and awareness of how different decisions may influence project dynamics and team interactions in diverse workplace settings.
